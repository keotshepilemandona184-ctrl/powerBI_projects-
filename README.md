## Regional Sales Performance Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales distribution across regions to identify performance trends, customer distribution, and profitability patterns.

The report highlights:
- Regional contribution to total sales and profit
- Customer distribution by region
- Product category performance across regions
- Sales trends over time
- Key drivers influencing sales performance

Key Insight:
The Central region generates the highest sales, while the East region has the largest customer base, suggesting differences between revenue concentration and customer volume.

Tools Used:
Power BI, Data Modeling, DAX Measures, Data Visualization, Business Insight Analysis
